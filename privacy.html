Privacy Policy

Data Collection

TechClever does not collect personal data. We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or profiling.

Analytics

We use GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and is fully GDPR compliant. It only counts page views in aggregate.

Local Storage

We store your language preference in your browser's local storage (key: tc-lang). This data never leaves your device and is only used to remember your chosen language.

External Links

This site contains links to external tools, products, and services. When you click an external link, that site's own privacy policy applies. We have no control over external sites' data practices.

Third-Party Services

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical data such as IP addresses for security purposes. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
